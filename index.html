The Real-World Problem

Students face difficulties in finding relevant study groups or qualified tutors that match their subject needs, learning goals, and availability. Existing platforms only offer basic search and filter options, lacking personalization, intelligence, and local relevance.

Limitations in Current Solutions

No Effective Matching

No effective way to match students with others nearby who share the same academic interests or learning pace.

Difficult to find peer with similar interests and learning goals

Lack of Quality Metrics

Tutors are often listed without quality metrics based on real student feedback.

Manual Coordination

Manual coordination wastes time and lowers learning engagement.
